### Project Description
SkillUp is a college-focused peer learning and collaboration platform designed to help students connect, share knowledge, and solve doubts in a structured digital environment.
The platform enables students to interact through a clean, ChatGPT-style interface where they can participate in discussions and contribute to academic conversations. One of the core features of SkillUp is its anonymous doubt system, which allows students to ask questions without revealing their identity. This encourages open communication, especially for students who hesitate to ask questions publicly.
Students can:
Create an account and log in securely
Participate in real-time style discussions
Post anonymous academic doubts
Reply to doubts and help peers
View structured conversation threads
SkillUp is built using modern web technologies including React (Vite) for frontend and Supabase for backend and database management, ensuring a fast, scalable, and cloud-connected experience.

### The Problem statement
Students hesitate to ask doubts publicly due to fear of judgment.
Many academic questions remain unanswered in classrooms.
No safe anonymous platform to ask ‚Äúbasic‚Äù or ‚Äúsimple‚Äù doubts.
No centralized digital space dedicated to peer-to-peer learning inside colleges.
Students with strong skills (coding, design, math, etc.) have no proper platform to share their knowledge.
Other students don‚Äôt know who to approach to learn specific skills.
Skill sharing happens informally and inconsistently.
Introverted students struggle to participate in classroom learning.
Lack of collaboration across departments and academic levels.

### The Solution
SkillUp acts as a centralized digital academic hub exclusively for college students to discuss, share, and collaborate.
By observing discussions and replies, students can identify knowledgeable peers and connect for learning support.
SkillUp provides an Anonymous Doubt System, allowing students to ask questions without revealing their identity.
Anonymous posting + digital interface encourages equal participation without social pressure.
SkillUp builds a growing database of doubts, answers, and discussions that future students can refer to.
SkillUp solves hesitation, unstructured communication, and lack of peer skill-sharing by creating a centralized, anonymous, and collaborative academic platform for college students.
